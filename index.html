About Twisk

My name is Sanjay Twisk and I’am a Rotterdam based freelance creative developer with a passion for interaction and music. Using my background in Communication & Multimedia Design and my love for technology and coding, I try to fill the gap between interactive design and web development.

While most of my exprience is in Front-end Development, I try to be as allround as possible. I love it when a design comes to life and I’m always eager to work with new frameworks. When I’m not coding, I’m working on the next Stellår EP producing fresh and energetic techno tracks.
